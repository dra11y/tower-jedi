In the present day, in a galaxy closer than you think....

It is a period of galactic war. The evil Galactic Empire has positioned its formidable Defence Towers to protect the Empire's ultimate weapon, the DEATH STAR.

The Rebel Forces and Princess Leia are counting on YOU to remove the enemy towers so that we can destroy the DEATH STAR and save the galaxy....

Welcome, Tower Jedi! To complete your mission, turn on your speakers and click the
New Game button to begin. You must manually target and destroy the green Defence Towers before they destroy your red XWing ships (targets shown in blue). Desktop or laptop forces will be needed to complete this mission, as our firing system does not yet work on mobile.
